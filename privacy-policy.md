# Privacy Policy for Streamline

**Last updated: [INSERT DATE BEFORE PUBLISHING]**

## Overview

Streamline ("the app") is a personal budgeting and debt payoff planning app. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

**The short version: Streamline does not collect, transmit, or share any of your personal or financial data. Everything you enter stays on your device.**

## What data the app stores

Streamline stores the following information **locally on your device only**, using your device's built-in storage:

- Paycheck amount, frequency, and pay dates
- Budget categories and amounts you create
- Debt information you enter (balances, interest rates, payment amounts, due dates)
- App preferences (theme selection, settings)
- Onboarding completion status

This data is stored using standard local storage technology built into your device's operating system. It is **never transmitted to any server**, because Streamline does not have a server or backend of any kind. There is no account system, no login, and no cloud sync.

## What data the app does NOT do

- We do not collect your name, email address, or any contact information
- We do not collect or transmit your financial data to us or anyone else
- We do not use analytics or tracking tools
- We do not share data with third parties, because we do not collect any data to share
- We do not use your data for advertising
- We do not have access to your data at all — it never leaves your device

## Data security

Because your data is stored only on your device and never transmitted anywhere, its security depends on the security of your own device (e.g., your device's lock screen, encryption settings). We recommend keeping your device's operating system and security settings up to date.

## Deleting your data

Since all data lives locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by:
- Using the app's built-in "Reset All Data" option in Settings, or
- Uninstalling the app, which removes all associated local data along with it

## Children's privacy

Streamline is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children, consistent with the fact that we do not collect information from anyone.

## Changes to this policy

If Streamline adds features in the future that change how data is handled (for example, an optional account or cloud sync feature), this policy will be updated accordingly, and the "Last updated" date above will reflect that change. Any such feature would only apply to users who explicitly opt in.
